# Mindful Earth — Photo Guide

Place your photos in this folder with EXACTLY these filenames:

HERO & MAIN
- hero-bg.jpg          → Full-width hero background (landscape, nature/forest/retreat scene)
- mission-photo.jpg    → Nature/earth/grounding image for Why We Exist section (portrait ratio)

ECOSYSTEM PILLARS
- ecosystem-community.jpg   → People gathering, community, connection
- ecosystem-education.jpg   → Books, journaling, learning, nature study
- ecosystem-experiences.jpg → Retreat, yoga, outdoor gathering
- ecosystem-media.jpg       → Photography, creative work, brand imagery

PRACTITIONERS
- practitioner-montana.jpg  → Your practitioner photo (warm, personal, atmospheric)
- practitioner-tammy.jpg    → Tammy's photo (ask her to send one)

FOUNDER SECTION
- founder-photo.jpg         → Your founder/atmospheric portrait (not corporate)

JOURNAL ARTICLES
- article-healing-activism.jpg      → Nature, earth, hands in soil
- article-practitioner-loneliness.jpg → Solo person, contemplative, honest
- article-retreat-ripple.jpg        → Retreat scene, group, nature gathering

TIPS:
- Optimise all images to WebP or compressed JPEG before uploading
- Aim for under 500KB per image for fast loading
- Landscape photos: minimum 1400px wide
- Portrait photos: minimum 800px wide
