## TIPS
- Compress all images before uploading — aim for under 400KB each
- Convert to WebP format if possible (better quality, smaller file)
- Landscape images: minimum 1400px wide
- Portrait images: minimum 800px wide
- You can use your own photography from retreats and events
- Unsplash.com (free) is a good source for nature/wellness placeholder images while you gather your own
