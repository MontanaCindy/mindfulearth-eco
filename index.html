Why We Exist

When you heal,
everything around
you does too.

Wellness has become a luxury. A monthly yoga membership can cost over £100. A retreat feels out of reach for most people. And yet healing is not a privilege — it is a human need.

Mindful Earth exists because we know what happens when people are given access to spaces that nourish them. They show up differently — for their families, their communities, for the planet itself.

This is the ripple we are building. One healed person at a time.

The Ginkgo
Why the Ginkgo

Ancient. Resilient. Returning.

The ginkgo is one of the oldest living species on earth — unchanged for over 200 million years. It survived the mass extinction that erased the dinosaurs. It was the only tree left standing after Hiroshima.

We chose it as our symbol because it embodies everything Mindful Earth believes: that resilience is our nature, that healing is ancient wisdom, and that when we return to the earth — we remember who we are.

Meet the Founder

I know what it feels like
to be disconnected.

From myself. From my community. From the planet. Anxiety brought me to yoga, and yoga brought me home. But the path was not easy — and it was not free.

I built my practice through energy exchanges, community generosity, and the grace of people who believed healing should be accessible. Without that, I would not be who I am today.

From running retreats in Canada to building a wellness community across the UK and beyond, I have seen what happens when people are given the space to return to themselves.
