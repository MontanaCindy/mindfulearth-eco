About Mindful Earth

A wellness ecosystem
in development.

Mindful Earth is a wellness platform focused on making wellness more accessible while supporting the practitioners who create it. At its core, it exists to strengthen connection to self, community, and nature — and to help build a more supportive ecosystem around how wellness is accessed and delivered.

Right now, we are in an early development phase, building the foundations of a connected wellness ecosystem. This includes:

  • Event planning and marketing support for wellness experiences
  • Branding, positioning, and design for practitioners and wellness brands
  • Networking and collaboration opportunities within the wellness space
  • Wellness experiences such as workshops, retreats, and community events

Mindful Earth is being built intentionally and gradually. If you are a practitioner, founder, venue, or organisation in wellness or conscious business — we are always open to connecting.

Why We Exist

When you heal,
everything around
you does too.

Wellness is not easy to access, nor affordable for many people. And yet healing is not a privilege — it is a human need.

The planet is at the core of this work. How we care for ourselves and how we care for the earth are not separate questions. Mindful Earth exists at that intersection — holding space for personal healing, community connection, and genuine environmental responsibility.

The Community Access Fund seeks to eliminate financial barriers to wellness. Because when people are given access to spaces that nourish them, they show up differently — for their families, their communities, and for the planet itself.

Our Symbol
The Ginkgo

Ancient. Resilient.
Returning.

The Ginkgo is one of the oldest living tree species on earth, largely unchanged for over 200 million years. It survived the mass extinction that wiped out the dinosaurs, and is known for its resilience in extreme environments — including being one of the first trees to regrow after Hiroshima.

I chose it because it felt relevant to what I'm building. Mindful Earth is something I'm building slowly and intentionally, with a focus on structure and clarity within the wellness space. At its core, this work is about connection to self, community, and nature — and building something that supports that in a more practical and accessible way.

The Ginkgo felt like a simple reflection of that.

Meet the Founder

Montana King

Wellness Professional · Founder · Somatic Practitioner · Yoga Teacher

I'm a wellness professional and founder with a background spanning science, somatic practice, and business development — and a growing conviction that the wellness space needs better infrastructure, not just better content.

Over the past several years I've facilitated breathwork, bodywork, and yoga, hosted retreats internationally, and worked with wellness practitioners and brands on growth, positioning, and experience design.

What I keep coming back to is this: demand for wellness is strong and growing, but the infrastructure connecting people to it — and supporting the practitioners delivering it — hasn't kept pace. Mindful Earth is my response to that.

The Community Access Fund

Wellness belongs
to everyone.

Wellness is not easy to access, nor affordable for many. The Mindful Earth Community Access Fund seeks to eliminate that barrier — supporting practitioners who want to offer subsidised sessions, and individuals who need supported access to wellness.

One hundred percent of contributions go directly to funding access. Practitioners in the fund are always paid in full — Mindful Earth covers the gap.
