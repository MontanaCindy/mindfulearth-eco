Work With Us

Come build something
that matters.

Mindful Earth is growing — and we are looking for aligned humans to grow with us. Not employees. Collaborators. People who believe in what we are building and want to contribute their craft to it.

See Open Roles

Please note: All roles at Mindful Earth at this stage are subcontractor positions — project-based, flexible, and values-aligned. We are not hiring employees. We are building a network of trusted collaborators who share our mission and want to grow alongside the platform.

How to apply: Send your expression of interest to montana@mindfulearth.eco with your role of interest in the subject line. Please include a Google Drive link to a 1–2 minute video of yourself sharing who you are and why you would like to work with Mindful Earth. Applications without a video will not be considered.

Open Roles

Who we are
looking for.

We are at an early and exciting stage of building Mindful Earth. The people we bring in now will help shape the culture, the quality, and the direction of what this becomes. We take that seriously — and we hope you do too.

Subcontractor · Flexible

Social Media Manager

We are looking for someone who understands wellness, speaks authentically online, and can help grow the Mindful Earth presence across Instagram and beyond. You will work across our accounts — @mindfulearth.eco, @mindfulearth.events, and @mindfulearth.media — with a consistent voice and genuine understanding of the community we are building.

What We Look For

Beyond the skill set — the person behind it.

Mission alignment

You genuinely believe wellness should be accessible, practitioners should be supported, and that business and integrity are not opposites.

Practitioner sensibility

You understand the work — not just the business of it. You have lived experience of what it means to show up for people in a meaningful way.

Integrity over performance

You would rather be honest than impressive. You do what you say you will do. You communicate clearly and take your commitments seriously.

Collaborative spirit

You work well with others, share credit generously, and understand that the best things are built together — not in isolation.

We are a small, intentional team at an early stage. The people who join now will help shape what Mindful Earth becomes. We do not take that lightly — and we hope you will not either.
