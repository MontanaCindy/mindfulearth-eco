The Mindful Earth Community Access Fund

Healing for those who
need it most.

The Mindful Earth Community Access Fund is being built with intention — a structure that will support practitioners who want to offer subsidised sessions and individuals who need supported access to wellness. The fund is in its early stages. If this resonates with you — as a potential contributor, practitioner, or future recipient — we would love to hear from you.

Register Your Interest Contribute to the Fund
The Ethos

Why this fund exists.

We have seen, firsthand, how transformative wellness support can be — and how arbitrarily access to it is distributed. A breakthrough breathwork session. A week-long retreat. A somatic session with the right practitioner. These things truly change lives.

And they are out of reach for too many people.

The Community Access Fund is our attempt to address that — not as a side project, but as a core mechanism of how Mindful Earth operates. Every transaction on the platform contributes. Every application is treated with genuine care.

What it covers

Two ways to apply

  • For practitioners — Apply to offer free or subsidised sessions, classes, or retreats through the fund. We cover the gap so you are always paid in full.
  • For individuals — Apply for supported access to practitioners, retreat experiences, courses, and self-guided resources within the Mindful Earth network.
  • No minimum threshold — If cost is a genuine barrier, that is enough.
  • Rolling applications — We review regularly and respond honestly about availability.
Register Your Interest

You are worth reaching out for.

There is no minimum threshold of "need." If you feel you would benefit and the cost is a genuine barrier, that is enough. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis — we will always be honest with you about what is available.
