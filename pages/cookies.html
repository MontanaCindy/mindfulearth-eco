Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Mindful Earth (mindfulearth.eco)

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use them.

How We Use Cookies

Mindful Earth uses a minimal number of cookies to ensure the site works properly and to understand how visitors use it. We do not use advertising cookies or sell your data to third parties.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies — necessary for the website to function. These cannot be disabled.
  • Preference cookies — remember your choices (such as cookie consent). Stored locally on your device.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors interact with the site so we can improve it. Only used with your consent.

Third-Party Cookies

Some of our third-party services may set their own cookies:

  • Calendly — sets cookies when you use our booking system
  • Stripe / PayPal / Square — set cookies during payment processing

We do not control these third-party cookies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

You can also withdraw your cookie consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or contacting us at connect@mindfulearth.eco.
