Services

Wellness at work that actually works.

Genuine, well-designed wellness programming for teams and organisations — not a box-ticking exercise, but a real investment in the humans doing the work. Retreats, workshops, ongoing programming, and consulting for companies who take their people seriously.

Team Retreats Enquiry-based

Half-day, full-day, and multi-day retreat experiences designed for corporate teams. Includes movement, somatic practices, nature immersion, and facilitated connection. Every retreat is designed from scratch.

Workplace Wellness Workshops Enquiry-based

Nervous system regulation, stress resilience, embodied leadership, and mindful communication. In-person at your offices or online. 90 minutes to full-day formats available.

Wellness Strategy Consulting Enquiry-based

For HR and leadership teams building a genuine workplace wellness culture. Assessment, strategy, and implementation support — the full picture, not just a programme.

Common Questions

Before you reach out.

We've done corporate wellness before and it didn't land. Why would this be different?

Most corporate wellness fails because it's generic and disconnected from real working life. We design everything around your specific team and culture — and we're honest about what will and won't work for you.

How big does our team need to be?

We work with teams of all sizes — from a 6-person startup wanting a day retreat to a 200-person organisation building an ongoing wellness strategy. The approach scales.

Can you work with us virtually?

Yes. Virtual workshops, online resources, and remote facilitation are all available. We'll recommend what format works best for your goals.

What does pricing look like?

All corporate engagements are proposal-based — because the right scope for a 10-person team retreat is very different to an organisation-wide wellness strategy. Book a call and we'll put together something tailored.

your people?

Start with a free 30-minute call with our team. We'll understand your needs and be honest about whether and how we can help.
