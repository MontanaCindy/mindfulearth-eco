Our Eco Pledge

We are of the earth.
Not just named after it.

The .eco domain is not a marketing choice — it is a commitment. Mindful Earth holds itself accountable to the natural world that underpins everything we do and everything we believe in.

Venue & Partner Standards

Every venue and supplier in our network is assessed for environmental practices. We prioritise nature-based settings, low-impact operations, and locally sourced everything. Partners who do not meet our criteria are not listed.

Carbon Consideration

We are honest about the environmental cost of travel-based experiences and work to offset where we can, reduce where we should, and be transparent always. We will never greenwash. We will always be learning.

Digital Footprint

Our digital infrastructure is hosted on green servers. Our materials are digital-first. Our packaging, where physical products exist, is plastic-free. Small things, done consistently, matter.

The .eco Commitment

As a verified .eco domain holder, we are publicly committed to measurable environmental action. Our pledge is on record and updated annually. You can view our verified profile at profiles.eco/mindfulearth.

"We do not protect what we are disconnected from. We heal the earth by healing ourselves — and we heal ourselves by returning to it."
— Mindful Earth
