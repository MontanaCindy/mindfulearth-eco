End-to-end planning support from concept through to delivery. Venue sourcing, supplier coordination, run-of-show, guest experience design, and on-call support throughout. You focus on facilitating — we handle everything else.

Bespoke wellness programming for companies and brands. Team retreats, wellness days, brand activations, and conference wellness integration. Every programme is designed from scratch around your team's needs and your brand's values. Enquiry-based.

Common Questions

Before you reach out.

Do I need to have a venue already?

Not at all. Venue sourcing is one of the core parts of what we do. You tell us the kind of experience you want to create — we find the right space for it.

I'm a first-time retreat host. Is this right for me?

Absolutely. In fact, first-time hosts often get the most from working with us — because we can help you avoid the costly mistakes that derail most first retreats.

How far in advance should I get in touch?

For a full retreat, ideally 3-6 months before your intended date. For a day event or workshop, 6-8 weeks is usually enough. That said — get in touch wherever you are and we'll be honest about what's achievable.

What if the retreat doesn't sell out?

Marketing support is part of our planning packages. We work with you on filling your event — not just planning it. For our full Marketing service, see the Wellness Marketing page.
