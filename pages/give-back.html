Wellness should be
for everyone.

We built Mindful Earth with access at its core — not as an afterthought, but as a founding principle. Every purchase, every session, every retreat booked through Mindful Earth contributes to something larger than the transaction.

The Model

Access built into the design.

Because we know that the people who most need healing are often the ones least able to afford it.

A portion of every transaction on Mindful Earth is automatically routed to the Community Access Fund. This is not charity — it is design. It is what a truly ethical wellness ecosystem looks like.

The Community Access Fund →

By spending here

Every purchase on Mindful Earth — workbooks, sessions, retreats — routes a percentage to the Access Grant. Healing others is built into the model.

By applying for support

If you need support accessing wellness and cost is a genuine barrier, you can apply. No judgment. No unnecessary hoops. Just an honest conversation.

Register Your Interest

By contributing directly

If you're in a position to give, even a small contribution goes directly to supporting someone else's access to healing. 100% stays in the fund.
