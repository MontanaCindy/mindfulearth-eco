Common Questions

Before you reach out.

I don't have much of an audience yet. Can you still help?

Yes. In fact, building from a small base with the right strategy is often more effective than trying to fix bad habits with a large one. We'll build the right foundation from where you are.

Do I need to be on every social media platform?

Absolutely not. We'll help you identify where your audience actually is and build a sustainable presence there — rather than spreading yourself thin everywhere.

What's the difference between the Brand Audit and the Retainer?

The Brand Audit is a one-time deep review — great if you want clarity and a clear action plan you can implement yourself. The Retainer is ongoing support where we're in it with you every month.

How quickly will I see results?

Honest answer: marketing is a slow burn. You'll see movement within weeks but real traction takes 2-3 months of consistency. We'll be transparent about expectations from the start.

Retreat & Event
Marketing

Marketing a retreat or event is a completely different challenge to building a brand. You have a deadline. You need specific people in specific seats by a specific date. Generic social media advice won't get you there.

We have planned, marketed, and filled wellness retreats internationally. We know what moves the needle — and what wastes your time.

These are different disciplines. Brand building is slow and long-term. Event marketing is fast, targeted, and outcome-focused. We do both — and we know which one you need right now.
