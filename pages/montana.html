Mindful Earth Practitioner

Montana King

Somatic Practitioner · Yoga Teacher · Wellness & Business Consultant

I work at the intersection of the body, the breath, and the business of building a life that feels true. Whether we're moving together on the mat, working somatically through what the mind can't yet reach, or mapping a strategy for your wellness brand — my approach is the same: grounded, honest, and genuinely invested in your becoming.

I've spent six years hosting retreats, supporting practitioners, and building wellness experiences across three continents. I know what it takes to create something meaningful — and I know how much easier it is when you're not doing it alone.

Based in London. Working globally. Always rooted in nature.
