About Mindful Earth

We believe healing
is not a luxury. It is a right.

The origin, the belief, and the vision of a wellness ecosystem built on the conviction that when one person heals, the world shifts.

The Origin

How it began.

Mindful Earth was born in 2019 from a simple but persistent observation: the wellness world was fragmented, often inaccessible, and rarely connected to the deeper ecology of community and nature that makes healing sustainable.

Montana King — founder, retreat host, somatic practitioner, and lifelong student of the body and earth — began building retreats and experiences that tried to answer a different question. Not just "how do we feel better?" but "how do we come home to ourselves, and bring others with us?"

What started as a single retreat became a community. The community revealed a need for education. The education pointed toward a platform. The platform demanded a community access fund. And here we are — still building, still rooting deeper.

The Belief

What we know to be true.

We believe that when one person heals, it ripples outward — into their relationships, their community, and ultimately the world. That healing is not linear, not always comfortable, and not one-size-fits-all.

That nature is not a backdrop to wellness — it is the source of it. And that no one should be priced out of the support they need to come home to themselves.

The Vision

Where we are going.

Mindful Earth is becoming a living ecosystem — a place where practitioners are supported to build sustainable, values-led businesses; where seekers can find the right guide at the right moment; where experiences genuinely transform; and where a portion of every transaction quietly funds access for those who couldn't otherwise afford it.

We are at the beginning of something we can feel but not yet fully see. And we are glad you are here for it.

Our Beliefs

The things we will not waver on.

Healing is collective.

When you heal, the people around you do too. We are not designed to do this alone.

Access is non-negotiable.

Wellness cannot be only for those who can afford a £200 retreat. We build access into the model.

Nature is the teacher.

Before any framework, any modality, any system — there is the earth. We return there, always.

Practitioners need holding too.

The people doing the healing work deserve as much support as those receiving it.
