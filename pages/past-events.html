Autumn Harmony Retreat
Deux Rivières, Ontario

Deux Rivières, Ontario Autumn Equinox 2 Nights · Women's Retreat Ceremony + Yoga + Nature

Two days of soul-connecting in nature, gathered in celebration of the Autumn Equinox. A small, intimate circle of women — connecting, releasing, breathing, dancing, laughing, and nourishing together.

"Ever since I can remember I have loved hosting and bringing lovely souls together to enjoy the sweetness of life. My life is an expression of joy, love, adventure and self discovery. I am so excited to spend the weekend celebrating the autumn equinox, connecting, releasing, breathing, dancing, laughing, nourishing, and holding space for other women to connect to nature and find harmony in the changes that infinitely surrounds us."

— Montana King, Host
