The honest truth

The problem usually isn't your content.
It's your positioning.

You're posting. You're showing up. People seem interested — but they're not signing up. The instinct is to post more, try different platforms, or change your content style. Usually that's not the problem.

The problem is that your offer, your message, or your sign-up pathway isn't clear enough yet. This workbook works through all three — with simple, practical frameworks you can apply to your retreat, workshop, or event the same day.

You're a yoga teacher, somatic practitioner, breathwork facilitator, or energy healer whose sessions and events aren't filling consistently despite real interest

Positioning first. Marketing second.

This workbook starts with your offer and your message — because no amount of content strategy fixes unclear positioning. What's inside

01

Your Offering — Clarity First

Before marketing anything, we need clarity on the work itself. What are you actually offering and what transformation does it create?

02

Who It's For

Many offerings struggle because they're too broad. Clarity comes from specificity — who benefits most from this work, and what are they currently struggling with?

03

How You're Currently Communicating

An honest audit of your website, social posts, and event descriptions. Would someone unfamiliar understand within 5–10 seconds?

04

Simplifying Your Message

What is it? Who is it for? Why does it matter? Often less is more effective. This section helps you distil your message to its most powerful form.

05

Structuring Your Offer for Bookings

Most practitioners don't have a clear pathway from interest to booking. Map your client journey and remove the friction that's costing you bookings.

06

Next Steps — One Focus

Clarity without action changes nothing. This section helps you identify the one thing to focus on first — and actually do it.
