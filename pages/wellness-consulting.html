Common Questions

Before you reach out.

How is this different from other business coaching?

Most business coaching applies a generic framework regardless of industry. This is built specifically for wellness practitioners — by someone who has been one. We understand the real tensions: wanting to be accessible, needing to be sustainable, not wanting to feel like you're selling.

I'm just starting out. Is this for me?

Yes — and honestly, starting with the right structure is much easier than fixing a broken one later. A single strategy session early in your journey can save months of confusion.

What's the difference between a single session and the 3-month engagement?

A single session gives you clarity and a roadmap. The 3-month engagement is for practitioners who want sustained support as they implement — regular sessions, ongoing accountability, and a genuine partner in the process.

Do you only work with yoga teachers?

Not at all. We work with somatic practitioners, breathwork facilitators, coaches, energy healers, retreat hosts, wellness brands — anyone building in wellness.

The Philosophy Behind the Work

Honouring where
you are right now.

The wellness and business world is full of programmes that ask you to leap before you are ready — to scale before you have a foundation, to launch before you have clarity, to grow before you have learned to sustain what you have already built.

This is not that.

The Ginkgo did not survive 200 million years by growing fast. It survived by going deep — sending roots down further than anyone could see, long before it grew tall. That is the philosophy behind every piece of consulting work we do at Mindful Earth.

We believe that every stage of building has its own wisdom. The early stage is not a lesser version of the later stage — it is where the most important lessons live. The clarity you find in Phase 0 is what makes Phase 1 possible. The community you build slowly is the one that actually holds. The business model you test with 10 clients before you scale to 1,000 is the one that actually works.

Trusting the timing is not passive. It is one of the most courageous and strategic things a founder can do.

This philosophy is baked into how we work. We will not push you to the next stage before you are ready. We will help you honour where you are — and build from there, with intention and with roots.
